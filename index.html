01 · The Problem

The sales process ended
where the real risk began.

Closing a deal in HubSpot was only the beginning. Everything after "Closed Won" depended entirely on a person remembering to act — and acting correctly every time.

01Manually copy contact email from HubSpot into a separate inbox
02Find the right welcome email template and adapt it for the client
03Send it — and hope the right version was sent to the right person
04Update the deal record in HubSpot (or forget entirely)
05Log it somewhere — a spreadsheet, a sticky note, or nothing
What this actually caused
3–5 customers per month received no welcome email
Duplicate emails sent to the same contact during busy periods
HubSpot deal records left incomplete — no audit trail
5–10 minutes of low-value admin work lost per deal closed
No way to know whether a customer had been contacted or not
02 · The Solution

An event-driven pipeline that
owns the entire handoff process.

I replaced five manual steps with a single automated trigger. The moment a deal stage changes to Closed Won in HubSpot, the system takes full ownership — fetching data, validating it, deduplicating, sending, updating, and logging.

  • Fetches deal and contact data from HubSpot — no copy-paste, no human memory required
  • Validates data quality before acting — checks that a contact exists and the email address is present
  • Deduplicates against Google Sheets — uses deal_id as a unique key, preventing any repeat processing of the same deal
  • Sends a professional welcome email via Brevo — triggered automatically with the correct contact data
  • Writes back to HubSpot — sets handoff_sent = yes so the deal record is always up to date
  • Logs every outcome to Google Sheets — with status, timestamp, deal ID, and contact email for full traceability
  • Routes every failure to the internal team — nothing fails silently; the right person is notified immediately

05 · Challenges Solved

The decisions that make
this system production-grade.

Connecting tools is straightforward. Making the system reliable under real-world conditions is where the design thinking happens.

Challenge

Webhook retries cause duplicate emails

Make.com webhooks can fire more than once for the same event. Without a guard, every retry would re-send the welcome email to the same customer.

Solution: Before any action is taken, the system checks Google Sheets for the incoming deal_id. If it already exists, the scenario stops immediately. The deduplication layer is the first real operation in the pipeline — not an afterthought.
Challenge

Silent failures are worse than no automation

If any step fails and nothing happens, the business doesn't know a customer was missed. The old manual process at least had a human who might notice.

Solution: Every failure path — missing contact, invalid email, failed Brevo call, failed HubSpot write — triggers both a log entry with error type and an immediate internal team notification. No failure is silent.
Challenge

HubSpot contacts aren't always complete

Sales reps close deals against contacts that may be incomplete — no email, no contact linked at all. Attempting to send without validating produces API errors and gaps in the log.

Solution: The pipeline validates both contact existence and email address presence before attempting any downstream action. Each failure type is logged with a distinct status code for easy diagnosis.
Challenge

No traceability after the fact

The original process had no record of what was sent, when, or to whom. Any follow-up question — "did this customer receive the email?" — required manual investigation.

Solution: Every run — successful or not — is written to Google Sheets with deal ID, contact email, outcome status, and timestamp. The sheet becomes a full audit trail the team can query at any time.

07 · Why This Matters

What this system

Anyone can connect two tools with a webhook. The quality of automation work shows in how you handle the parts that go wrong.

01 · Problemet

Säljprocessen slutade
där den verkliga risken började.

Att stänga en affär i HubSpot var bara första steget. Allt efter "Closed Won" berodde helt på att en person kom ihåg att agera — och agerade rätt varje gång.

01Manuellt kopiera kontaktens e-post från HubSpot till ett annat fönster
02Hitta rätt välkomstmall och anpassa den för kunden
03Skicka det — och hoppas att rätt version gick till rätt person

05Logga det någonstans — ett kalkylblad, en lapp eller ingenstans
Vad detta faktiskt ledde till
3–5 kunder per månad fick inget välkomstmejl
Dubbla mejl skickades till samma kontakt under hektiska perioder
HubSpot-affärer lämnades ofullständiga — ingen revisionshistorik
5–10 minuters lågvärdigt administrativt arbete bortkastat per affär

02 · Lösningen

En händelsestyrd pipeline som
äger hela överlämningsprocessen.

Jag ersatte fem manuella steg med en enda automatisk trigger. I det ögonblick en affär ändrar status till Closed Won i HubSpot tar systemet fullt ansvar.

  • Hämtar affärs- och kontaktdata från HubSpot — ingen kopiering, inget mänskligt minne krävs
  • Validerar datakvalitet innan åtgärd — kontrollerar att kontakt och e-postadress finns
  • Deduplicerar mot Google Sheets — använder deal_id som unik nyckel för att förhindra dubbelbehandling
  • Skickar välkomstmejl via Brevo — triggas automatiskt med rätt kontaktdata
  • Uppdaterar HubSpot — sätter handoff_sent = yes för att hålla affärsposten aktuell
  • Loggar varje utfall till Google Sheets — med status, tidsstämpel, deal-ID och kontaktmejl
  • Dirigerar varje fel till internt team — ingenting misslyckas utan att rätt person notifieras

02 · Rešenje

Događajno-pokretana pipeline koja
preuzima ceo proces predaje.

Zamenila sam pet ručnih koraka jednim automatskim okidačem. Čim se status posla promeni u Closed Won u HubSpot-u, sistem preuzima punu odgovornost.

  • Preuzima podatke o poslu i kontaktu iz HubSpot-a — bez kopiranja, bez oslanjanja na pamćenje
  • Validira kvalitet podataka pre akcije — proverava da kontakt i email adresa postoje
  • Deduplikuje u odnosu na Google Sheets — koristi deal_id kao jedinstveni ključ
  • Šalje welcome email putem Brevo — automatski sa tačnim podacima kontakta
  • Ažurira HubSpot — postavlja handoff_sent = yes
  • Beleži svaki ishod u Google Sheets — sa statusom, vremenskim pečatom, deal ID i email adresom
  • Rutira svaku grešku do internog tima — ništa ne pada tiho

Ako korak ne uspe bez obaveštenja, firma ne zna da je kupac propušten.

Rešenje: Svaka putanja greške — nedostajući kontakt, nevažeći email, neuspelo Brevo slanje — okida i log unos i trenutno obaveštenje timu.
Izazov

HubSpot kontakti nisu uvek potpuni

Prodavci zatvaraju poslove sa kontaktima koji mogu nemati email adresu. Pokušaj slanja bez validacije proizvodi API greške.

Rešenje: Pipeline validira i postojanje kontakta i prisustvo email adrese pre ikakve akcije.
Izazov

Nema traga nakon činjenice

Originalni proces nije imao evidenciju o tome šta je poslato, kada i kome.

Rešenje: Svako pokretanje — uspešno ili ne — upisuje se u Google Sheets sa deal ID, email adresom, statusom ishoda i vremenskim pečatom.
